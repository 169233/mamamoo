마마무 Mamamoo

K-pop female group

Mamamoo is a South Korean girl group formed by RBW, consisting of members Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein, and Hwasa. They are debuted in 2014 with their single "Mr. Ambiguous". They are recognized for their retro, jazz, R&B concepts and their strong vocal performances.
